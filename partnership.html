Partnership

Partnership with Genesis Core Systems.

Genesis pairs best-in-class partner technology with the modules we've built ourselves. The result is one integrated platform — not a marketplace, not a stitched-together vendor stack — shaped around how community banks actually operate.

Why partner with Genesis

One unified system, simple to run.

Genesis is tuned for the operating shape of a community bank: a lean IT team that has to keep the lights on while shipping change. The processes, defaults, and integrations are built around how community institutions actually run — not retrofitted from products designed for Tier-1 scale.

Our partnerships with major technology vendors keep that current. Regulatory updates, payments standards, security primitives — all of it comes into the platform without an upgrade project on the bank's side.

Design Partner Program

Become a Genesis Design Partner.

Genesis Core Systems invites forward-thinking community banks to join our exclusive Genesis Design Partner program. This initiative is designed for banks committed to leveraging technology to redefine the banking experience. With very limited enrollment, this program offers a unique opportunity to influence and pioneer the next generation of banking solutions.

Genesis is delighted to offer interested banks the opportunity to connect and gain access to a wealth of resources, including referrals, technology briefings, and direct contact with our leadership team.

  • 01

    Exclusive participation and influence

    Design Partners enjoy the privilege of serving on the Design Committee for Genesis products. This role allows you to provide guidance and have a direct impact on product development, ensuring that the solutions developed meet the specific needs of community banks like yours.

  • 02

    Customized technological solutions

    Receive a solution tailored specifically to your bank's needs, without the burden of funding common system requirements. This bespoke approach allows you to integrate unique functionalities at cost, ensuring that your bank retains its competitive edge.

  • 03

    Cost-effective upgrades and operations

    Design Partners benefit from the ability to migrate to new systems and include specific requirements at minimal cost. Operations are streamlined and enhanced, also offered at cost, which allows for greater financial control and forecasting accuracy.

Strategic Benefits

Three advantages that compound.

Direct product influence

Shape the features and capabilities of Genesis solutions directly. Your priorities become roadmap priorities, not feedback that vanishes into a black box.

Customization at cost

Implement customized solutions without the typical financial burden. Build the things your bank actually needs and pay only for the work itself.

Operational efficiency

Enjoy the simplicity and cost-effectiveness of migrating and operating with Genesis. Fewer vendors, fewer integration surfaces, fewer surprises.

Join the commitment

A collaborative environment where your bank can thrive.
