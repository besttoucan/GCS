Frequently Asked Questions

Questions, answered.

If you don't see your question here, drop us a note. We respond to every inquiry within one business day.

What kind of institutions does Genesis serve?
Genesis is purpose-built for community banks, credit unions, regional lenders, and specialty finance institutions. The platform scales across the range without losing features.
What makes Genesis different?
Three differences: a real-time event-driven architecture instead of nightly batch, open APIs across every module rather than walled gardens, and consolidated pricing aligned with deposit volume rather than per-seat licensing.
How long does implementation take?
Typical full core engagements run roughly fourteen weeks across four phases — discovery and design, build and integrate, migrate and cutover, stabilize and expand. Module-only adoption can be considerably shorter.
Can Genesis integrate with existing systems?
Yes. Genesis is modular and API-first. Many institutions start with one or two modules alongside their incumbent core and migrate further as they're ready.
What is your security and compliance posture?
SOC 2 controls, encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access with full audit logging, continuous compliance monitoring, and dedicated security review for each customer deployment. Security documentation is available under NDA on request.
What support do you provide after go-live?
Round-the-clock monitoring across every Genesis instance, a dedicated response team with documented incident playbooks, a named technical account manager, and quarterly business reviews. Patching and maintenance run on zero-downtime windows.
How does the Design Partner Program work?
Limited enrollment per cycle. Partners sit on the Genesis Design Committee and help shape the product roadmap, receive customized features without bearing the development cost, and benefit from cost-effective upgrades and operations. See Partnership for details.
What is your pricing model?
Predictable pricing aligned with deposit volume rather than seat count, with no enterprise-tier gatekeeping — every institution gets the full feature set. Specifics are shared under NDA during a scoping conversation.
How can I see the platform in action?
Book a working session through our contact page. We'll demo live, map your current stack against Genesis modules, and walk through what a phased adoption would look like.
How do I reach Genesis?
Email info@genesiscoresystems.com or call +1 (914) 477-CORE. Every inquiry reaches a real person.
