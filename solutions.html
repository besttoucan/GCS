Genesis Core Banking System

Our core banking system, initially designed for Tier-1 banks, is now uniquely tailored for community banks. Genesis brings sophisticated solutions within reach, featuring real-time processing, seamless API integration, and compliance management tools tailored to the needs of local communities.

Enjoy a user-friendly interface that streamlines operations, with quick access and simplified transactions, making banking intuitive for staff and customers alike. Our integration capabilities ensure your bank remains at the technological forefront, fostering growth without leaving behind the culture that makes your community bank special. With Genesis, leverage big bank tech for local bank success.

  • Real-time ledger with sub-second posting
  • Event-driven architecture for instant reconciliation
  • Open API for every entity
  • Native support for ACH, wire, real-time payments, and cards
